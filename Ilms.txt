# Launchsmith Portfolio - AI Agent Site Description

## Site Overview
Launchsmith is a UK-based web and mobile developer portfolio website showcasing professional development services and past projects. The site is built with Next.js 14 and serves as a marketing platform for Jake Dale-Rourke, a developer based in Longridge, Preston.

## Purpose
- Showcase development services (websites, web apps, mobile apps, automation/AI)
- Display portfolio of completed projects
- Provide information about the developer
- Generate leads through contact forms
- Share industry insights through blog posts

## Target Audience
- Startup founders
- Small business owners in the UK
- Companies needing web or mobile development
- Individuals with app ideas
- Businesses in Preston, Manchester, Liverpool, Leeds, London, and nationwide

## Important Pages

### Homepage (/)
- Landing page with hero section
- Featured projects showcase
- Client testimonials with project links
- Services overview
- Contact call-to-action
- Newsletter signup

### Portfolio (/portfolio)
- Lists all completed projects
- Filterable by category (Website, Web App, iOS App, SEO Monitoring)
- Featured projects section
- More projects section
- Each project card links to individual project detail pages

### Portfolio Detail Pages (/portfolio/[slug])
- Individual project case studies
- Includes: problem, solution, highlights, tech stack, gallery
- External links to live projects
- Contact CTA for similar projects
- Projects include: RankAlert, BulkZip, Tournament Generator, AutoCare Preston, and more

### Services (/services)
- Lists 4 main service categories with pricing
- Each service has description, outcomes, and included features
- FAQ section
- "Find out more" buttons link to individual service pages
- Contact CTA

### Individual Service Pages
- /services/websites - Website development details (£250-£8,000)
- /services/web-apps - Web application development (£1,000-£15,000)
- /services/mobile-apps - iOS/mobile app development (£500-£30,000)
- /services/automation - Automation & AI integration (£1,000-£15,000)

### About (/about)
- Personal introduction to Jake Dale-Rourke
- Work process (5-step workflow)
- Social proof links (GitHub, LinkedIn)
- Location: Longridge, Preston, UK

### Blog (/blog)
- Lists 5 SEO-focused blog posts
- Categories: Pricing, Mobile, Web Development, SEO, Accessibility
- Each post links to individual blog page
- Newsletter signup form

### Individual Blog Pages
- /blog/how-much-does-a-website-cost-in-uk
- /blog/ios-vs-android-app-development
- /blog/next-js-for-business-websites
- /blog/seo-fundamentals-for-uk-businesses
- /blog/building-accessible-websites

### Contact (/contact)
- Contact form for enquiries
- Form uses FormSpree for submission
- Success redirect to /thanks

### Thanks (/thanks)
- Thank you page after form submission
